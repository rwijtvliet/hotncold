This app visualises the temperature records for a few locations.

Why

Oftentimes, you hear or read statements like 'this is the warmest 1st of November on record', which by many is taken as an indication that the climate is getting warmer. At the same time, record-breaking low temperatures are also occasionally measured, which are then used by others to 'disprove' global warming.

I thought it'd be an interesting exercise to visualise these records, and show for each of the 366 days of the year, both the year in which the highest temperature, and that in the lowest temperature (on record) were measured.

Disclaimer

This is not rigorous science. Trends in temperature and climate are complex and cannot be reduced to the occurrence of extreme values. A much more useful visualisation, for example, is plotting the average temperature for all years, and see the trend line from there. Also, global warming might in some places result in 'local cooling', so looking at a single geographic location must not be representative of a larger area.
